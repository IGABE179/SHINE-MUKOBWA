Shine Umukobwa Project

Shine Umukobwa is a project that aims to empower rural teenage girls by providing them with access to learning resources, mentorship, and confidence building opportunities. The project is based in Rwanda and is supported by the Shine Foundation.

Project Objectives

  • Empower rural teenage girls to discover their purpose and potential
  • Provide access to quality education, training, and resources
  • Foster a sense of community and belonging among girls
  • Build confidence and self-esteem through mentorship and support
